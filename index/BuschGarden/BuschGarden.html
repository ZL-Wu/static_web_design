Introduction

Busch Gardens Tampa (formerly known as Busch Gardens Africa and Busch Gardens: The Dark Continent) is a 335-acre (136 ha) African-themed animal theme park located in the city of Tampa, Florida. Busch Gardens Tampa is owned and operated by SeaWorld Parks & Entertainment and is their 2nd largest park in terms of attendance behind SeaWorld Orlando. The park officially features eight roller coasters and two water rides amongst other rides and animal attractions. Its newest roller coaster, Cobra's Curse, opened in June 2016.

Programs

Rides and Roller Coasters

FLORIDA'S BEST THRILL RIDES
Explore Florida’s favorite coasters and thrill rides, right here in Tampa Bay. Whether you’re a coaster enthusiast or want rides that are fun for the whole family, explore Florida’s best coasters, right here in Tampa Bay. Take on a thrilling triple-launch coaster, a family spin coaster that’s a new ride every time, epic coaster legends and more.

World-Class Shows

FAMILY FRIENDLY SHOWS TAKE THE STAGE DAILY
Enjoy live shows from world-class performers – along with a few rescued rising stars. Your admission is your ticket to world-class entertainment, with live shows for all ages. Enjoy the laugh-out-loud antics of some rescued animal stars during Opening Night Critters, the breathtaking performances on ice during Turn It Up!, the fast face of Push Play plus the kid-friendly fun of Let's Play Together! at Sesame Street® Safari of Fun.

Animal Experiences

MAKE AN INSPIRING CONNECTION
Explore one of the largest, most highly accredited zoo facilities in the US. From some of the world’s most interesting animals to some of its most endangered, share in our passion for the animals in our care through up-close encounters and our state-of-the-art Animal Care Center, as seen on the hit TV series The Wildlife Docs.

Events

LOOKING FOR NEW THINGS TO DO IN TAMPA BAY?
From food festivals & live concerts to spectacular Halloween & Christmas events, explore exciting things to do in Tampa Bay. You’ll always find something fun going on at Busch Gardens. From our popular spring Food & Wine Festival to Summer Nights parties or Sesame Street® Kids' Weekends to our chilling Howl-O-Scream® or festive Christmas Town celebration. The best part? Many are included free with your admission.

IMMERSIVE TOURS, ENRICHING ENCOUNTERS

Trek across the Serengeti Plain® in an open-air vehicle. Go behind the scenes of our hit TV series The Wildlife Docs®. Book an Up-Close Tour, be a Keeper for the Day and more.
